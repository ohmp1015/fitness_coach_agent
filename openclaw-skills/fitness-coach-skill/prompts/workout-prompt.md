# Workout Generation Prompt

Generate a workout plan with this format:

## 📅 [Day] — [Body Part] Workout

### 🔥 Warm-up (5 min)
- Exercise 1
- Exercise 2

### 💪 Main Workout
1. **Exercise Name**
   - Sets: X | Reps: X | Rest: Xs
   - Form tip: brief instruction

### 🧊 Cool-down (5 min)
- Stretch 1
- Stretch 2

**Rules:**
- Match exercises to user's level and equipment
- Include proper warm-up and cool-down
- Add form tips for safety
- Be encouraging with emojis
